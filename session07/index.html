WEB ARTISTS

Pioneers and Contemporary Artists

5 WEB ARTISTS

  • One Terabyte of Kilobyte Age, Olia Lialina & Dragan Espenschied

    • This website caught my attention. I was drawn by the thumbnail on the Net Art Anthology it looks like president Bill Clinton in a bath. I love how its a website preserving multiple other peoples sites. It gives us an insight into the 90's early 2000's, and how people interacted with the web. I like the dog website on the page too the sound effects and the story is cute.

  • Automatic Rain, JODI

    • I immeditaley noticed this website due to seeing JODI's other work in previous sessions. I was intriuged by the title as well to see what something like that would be in website form. Sadly the website is down, its program is too old to run which is interesting to see how far we come with web site technology that older sites just dont work. I also just appreciate their work as it was early on in the webs life so it was impactful and shaped our web in some way today.

  • Love by Group Z, Belgium (Michaël Samyn)

    • I like this website as it presents a story and is like a video game with how the interacted elements connect to that. Sadly this work is also not up as its really old. The image of a persons shadow caught my eye as it flickered when i moved the page at all. I love how the artist uses their past experiences of love and relationships to create an immersive experience. Overall i really like this site, i wish it was up for me to explore.

  • Blacklash, by Mongrel

    • I like how this site is also another video game. It reminds me of something like f-zero on the snes with its retro gradient horizon. You can certainly tell its from the 90's as the assests of character models are 3d with shading and has that look, while a lot of modern logos and such are more bland and lack 3d aspects. I also find the quote abt how they felt about space invaders hilarious and how that made them want to create this.

  • The Dollie Clone Series by Lynn Hershman Leeson

    • This is another site that immeditaley caught my eye as I was looking at this at night and was spooked. Its an interesting idea having the cameras in the dolls eyes and putting it online. This work is defintely something i have not seen before. I love the detail of actually having the doll over the cam footage. However, its Extremely Creepy
